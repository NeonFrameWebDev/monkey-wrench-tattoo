What We Do

Ink and iron, by appointment.

Custom tattoos and body piercings, done right the first time. Cover-ups, touch-ups, fine line, traditional, realism, colour. Flash walls on three sides. Ear and nose piercings while you wait; deeper work by consult.

No walk-ins. Call the shop, text on the gram, or stop in to book a chair.

  1. 01

    Custom Tattoos

    Fine line, traditional, black & grey, colour, large-format custom. Bring a reference or bring a feeling. We'll draw the rest.

  2. 02

    Cover-Ups & Touch-Ups

    Old piece you regret? Faded ink from another decade? Book a consult and we'll plan a cover-up that earns its place.

  3. 03

    Ear & Nose Piercings

    Sterile, single-use needles. Full lobe and cartilage menu. Done in the back room with the pin-up on the wall.

  4. 04

    Body Piercings

    Navel, septum, nipple, surface. All by consult so we can place it right and send you home with an aftercare plan that works.

The Shop

Same block, same wrench.

We're not a chain, not a franchise, and we don't have a second location in some strip mall.

Monkey Wrench has been on Main Street for decades. The wall of flash gets rotated every few years. The stools at the counter have stayed the same. Regulars bring their kids in now. That's the pace we like.

If that's the kind of shop you want to sit in, call us. We'll pour you a coffee, look at your reference, and book you a chair.
